### Industry Outlook & Social Impact

1.  Why do AI Agents always seem to have "goldfish memory"? **LangChain**, in a [Blog Post (AI News)](https://x.com/shao__meng/status/1992407530123993337), points out that the root cause isn't the model itself, but rather outdated **context management** methods üí°. They've proposed a new paradigm: "context engineering," which involves letting Agents use a **file system** as an external workspace, just like programmers do. By reading and writing files, Agents can achieve persistent memory and precise information retrieval. This approach completely solves common ailments of traditional RAG like missed recalls and token explosion, truly empowering Agents with long-term memory and the ability to continuously evolve üöÄ.
